Biography

HI, I'M JAY — an electrical engineering student at Arizona State University with hands-on experience in embedded systems, rapid prototyping, and mechanical/electrical integration. I grew up in Livermore, California, where a kitchen-table fascination with circuits and code steadily became something more deliberate.

I am skilled in SOLIDWORKS, Arduino development, MATLAB, Python, and C++, and I have shipped real-world projects involving sensor calibration, CAD modeling, microcontroller programming, and full-stack web development.

I am currently seeking an engineering internship to apply these skills to real product development, with a preference for roles that sit at the intersection of hardware and software.

What drives me is simple: I want to build things that actually matter. Whether that means a braille display that helps a student in South Africa read the web, or a market simulation engine that makes a hackathon audience gasp — the goal is always the same: make it work, make it well.
