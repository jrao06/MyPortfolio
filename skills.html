Approach

On the Philosophy of Building

Asked to describe his working method, Mr. Rao is characteristically direct: "Start with the problem, not the technology. Pick the simplest tool that solves it, then iterate."

This pragmatism is evident across his portfolio. Dollar Dash did not require a sophisticated back-end — but real-time gameplay demanded WebSockets, and so he learned them. The braille display did not require CAD — but manufacturable parts did, and so SOLIDWORKS entered his toolkit.
